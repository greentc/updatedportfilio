My Projects

  • This summer

    This summer I will begin by going to Peru to work with volunteer firefighters in Lima, the largest city and capital of Peru. The trip is through a program here at the University of Michigan, GIEU. I discovered the program in October, and on a whim I decided to give it a shot and apply. While it was ultra competitive, I am Terrence Green and I am a winner, so naturally I got in. When I return from Peru, I will be heading to Los Angelos to begin my acting career. I like to think of myself as an up and coming Taylor Kitsch, or as I first discovered him, Tim Riggins.

  • Peru

    Peru is about to be the time of my life. I used to lifeguard when I was younger so when I saw that there was a program that would allow me to be able to work with firegihters who save lives on a daily basis. I knew it was for me. While in Peru I will do more than work with firefighters, I will bond with students from the University of Michigan. I have already become fast friends with a couple kids on the trip, so I know it will make for a memory to remember. Never say no to a memory.

  • LA

    Los Angelos? Yes I know it sounds crazy but my biggest dream has always been to be an actor. I love to immerse myself in the unfamiliar, to experience life through perspectives completely unlike my own. How better to do that than as an actor in Hollywood? I know it seems silly, but aren't most dreams? Better to try than not try and always wonder. Besides, if it does not work out, I can always come back to the University of Michigan in the fall.
